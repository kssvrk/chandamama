Chandamama

We Indians , term our moon as Chandamama, Earth's Brother. Incredible people at ISRO put Chandrayaan-2 in orbit and made the data open to all. This project brings sample datasets directly to you as explorable , interactive data after few essential processing steps.

Data Instrument Description
High Resolution Moon Orbiter High Resolution Camera - Chandrayaan2
3 Dimensional Lunar Crater Terrain Mapping Camera - Chandrayaan2
